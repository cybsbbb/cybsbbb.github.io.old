Biography


I am a second-year M.S. student in Computer Science at the University of Southern California (USC). Before that, I received my Bachelor degree in Computer Science from University of Science and Technology of China, Hefei in 2016, under the supervision of Prof. Yu Zhang.

My research interests are Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Mining, and Spatial Science. I have been working at Spatial Computing Lab for one year. I am mainly involved in building deep learning model for digital map text detection, building system that relate to spatial data mining with large-scale data. More information is available in my curriculum vitae.
